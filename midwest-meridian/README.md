# Midwest Meridian

**Single Release**

A Son Volt-inspired acoustic folk/alt-country track. Dusty highways, worn-in denim, heartland melancholy.

## Track Listing

1. **Midwest Meridian** - The title track capturing the spirit of the American heartland

## Style

- Alt-country / Americana / Folk
- Sparse, honest arrangements
- Acoustic-driven with pedal steel and fiddle
- Jay Farrar-era Son Volt influence ("Trace", "Straightaways")

## Instrumentation

- Acoustic Guitar (fingerpicked/strummed)
- Pedal Steel
- Fiddle
- Electric Guitar (clean Telecaster)
- Upright Bass
- Brushed Drums
- Vocals

## Production Notes

- Keep dynamics intact - don't over-compress
- Room sound should feel cohesive
- Warm, not loud
- Let instruments breathe
